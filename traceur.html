Meet the Traceur

Shaheed is currently a Parkour Tracuer, one who uses the urban environment as their own obstacle course, he started discovering the sport way back on 2004 and has never regretted it.

I remember the first time I discovered Parkour, it was entirely by accident - I was chasing cats across the Rooi plein and a buddy of mine told me that it looked similar to a video he'd seen on Youtube, he later showed me a video of David Belle doing a short Parkour run, and I've been hooked ever since.

2004-2013

Shaheed has since stopped practising Parkour, but will soon get back into the diving and rolling when his life isn't so busy anymore, we've included a bunch of pics to keep you happy.
